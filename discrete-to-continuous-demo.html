In pmf, height is the probability — exactly what's on Slide 4. Switch to Histogram: redraw those same numbers as touching bars, where height = probability ÷ width, so a bar's area is now the probability instead. Drag an interval, then grow n: heights shoot up and bars blur into a Curve, but the readout — an area — barely moves. That's the quantity a continuous pdf is built to track.
